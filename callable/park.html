throw_a_party(*args, **kwargs)¶

Throw a party at the park to gain happiness.

Requires 10,000 food, which will be used automatically, and produces a base of 3,000 happiness.

For each unique food type you have onsite (in quantities of at least 500), the happiness multiplier increases by one. So four food types increases your multiplier to 4, giving you: 3000 (base happy) * 4 (food multiplier) == 12,000 happy

Additionally, each level of the park awards an additional multiplier of 0.3. So at level 10, a party would give you: 3000 (base happy) * (10 * 0.3 == 3) (level multiplier) == 9,000 happy

Once a party has been thrown, it takes 12 hours to wind down, at which point you can throw another party.

Raises ServerError 1010 if there’s already a party going on.
