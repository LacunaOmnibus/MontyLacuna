view_foreign_ships(page_number: int=1, **kwargs)¶

Shows incoming ships, 25 at a time.

“Foreign” ships include all ships. Even if your empire is the owner of the station, and your empire is the one sending ships, those ships will show up as “foreign.”

Arguments:
  • page_number – Optional page of results to view. Defaults to 1.
Returns a tuple:
  • ships – list of lacuna.ship.IncomingShip objects.
  • number – total number of incoming ships.

view_ships_travelling(page_number: int=1, **kwargs)¶

Does not appear to do anything.

This method’s existence might be a copy/paste error. ALL ships incoming to a space station, even if they’re sent by the station owner, are shown by view_foreign_ships().

This method doesn’t display any incoming ships, regardless of owner, and you can’t send ships out from a Space Station. So this never returns anything but an empty list.

Included for API completeness.

Arguments:
  • page_number – Optional page of results to view. Defaults to 1.
Returns a tuple:
  • ships – list of lacuna.ship.IncomingShip objects.
  • number – total number of incoming ships.
